# Privacy Policy for Chess.com Sound Extension

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy describes how the Chess.com Sound Extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.** The Extension does not:
- Collect personally identifiable information
- Track your browsing history
- Monitor your chess games or results
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share data with third parties

## Data Storage

The Extension stores only two preferences locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:
- **enabled** (boolean): Whether sound effects are turned on or off
- **volume** (number): Your preferred volume level (0-100)

This data is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your device.

## Permissions Used

- **Host Permission (chess.com)**: Required to detect game events (piece captures, checkmate) on chess.com pages
- **Storage Permission**: Required to save your sound and volume preferences locally

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the Extension's store listing.
